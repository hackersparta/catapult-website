Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Catapult AI ("we", "our", "us") operates the website catapultai.in and provides AI-powered automation services including WhatsApp Business messaging, social media automation, and lead management solutions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business details when you reach out to us or use our services.
  • WhatsApp Messages: Messages sent to and received from our WhatsApp Business numbers for the purpose of providing automated responses and customer support.
  • Business Data: Information you share with us to set up automation workflows, including social media accounts, ad accounts, and business preferences.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services, including timestamps, message frequency, and feature usage.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address when you visit our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our AI automation services.
  • To send and receive WhatsApp Business messages on your behalf.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To improve our services and develop new features.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. WhatsApp Business Messaging

Our services integrate with the Meta WhatsApp Business Platform. When you or your customers interact with our WhatsApp-enabled services:

  • Messages are processed through Meta's WhatsApp Business API.
  • We may use AI to generate automated responses to incoming messages.
  • Message content is stored securely and used only for the purpose of providing the service.
  • We do not sell or share WhatsApp message content with third parties for advertising purposes.
  • You can opt out of automated messaging at any time by sending "STOP".

Our use of WhatsApp Business API complies with WhatsApp's Business Policy and Commerce Policy.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (Meta, OpenAI, cloud hosting providers) necessary to deliver our services.
